01 — About
“Architecture is not just building — it is the art of shaping human experience.”

I am Tannu Pandey, a first-year B.Arch student at SPA Chennai, passionate about biomimicry, spatial narratives, and the poetry of built form. My work explores the intersection of nature, culture, and the built environment.

From hand-drawn streetscapes and physical models to conceptual biomimetic designs, I believe architecture must be rooted in observation — of nature, of people, and of place.
